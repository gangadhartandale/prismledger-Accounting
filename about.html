About Prismledger

Professional, friendly accounting support for individuals and small businesses in BC.

Prismledger Accounting Service was created to make accounting and tax simple for everyday business owners. We know that most entrepreneurs don’t enjoy spreadsheets, reconciliations, or tax forms—but we do.


Whether you are running a small retail shop, restaurant, or professional practice, we focus on clear communication, reliable deadlines, and practical advice so you can make better decisions with your numbers.


Our services are designed to be flexible and affordable, so you can start small and add more support as your business grows.
