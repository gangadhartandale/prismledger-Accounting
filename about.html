About Prismledger

Clear, reliable, and affordable accounting support for individuals, newcomers, and small businesses.

Meet Gangadhar Tandale

Gangadhar is the Accounting Manager at Prismledger Accounting Services, serving individuals, newcomers, families, and small businesses across Surrey, Delta, and White Rock. He specializes in providing clear, reliable, and affordable accounting solutions with a focus on compliance, accuracy, and client care.

His experience spans tax filing, bookkeeping, payroll, audits, and CRA collections. He has worked with leading accounting firms including MNP LLP, Manning Elliott LLP, and Buckley Dodds CPA LLP.

His recent role with the Government of Canada as an Income Tax Collections Officer gave him first-hand insight into legal actions such as garnishments, liens, and enforcement measures — knowledge that he now uses to help clients avoid stressful CRA situations.

Gangadhar completed over 300 tax returns as a volunteer with the City of Vancouver’s Community Tax Clinic.

  • IBM Data Visualization & Dashboard Essentials
