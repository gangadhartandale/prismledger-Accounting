About Prismledger

Clear, reliable and affordable accounting support for individuals, newcomers and small businesses in Canada.

Meet Gangadhar Tandale

Gangadhar Tandale is the Accounting Manager at Prismledger Accounting Services, where he is dedicated to supporting individuals, newcomers and small business owners with clear, reliable and affordable accounting solutions. His approach is grounded in accuracy, integrity and a genuine commitment to helping clients stay compliant and confident in their financial responsibilities.

Gangadhar brings extensive experience across tax filing, bookkeeping, payroll, audit and collections, allowing him to understand both sides of compliance – the CRA perspective and the client experience. His background includes roles at respected accounting firms in Vancouver such as MNP LLP, Manning Elliott LLP and Buckley Dodds CPA LLP, where he gained hands-on experience serving a wide range of clients and industries.

He founded Prismledger Accounting Services with a mission to help individuals and small businesses who struggle with tax compliance, outstanding balances and collection challenges. His recent tenure with the Government of Canada as an Income Tax Collections Officer provided deep insight into how the CRA initiates legal actions, garnishments, liens and other enforcement measures. With this knowledge, he helps clients avoid stressful collection situations and resolve tax matters proactively and respectfully.

Gangadhar is currently preparing for the Chartered Professional Accountant (CPA) designation to further strengthen his technical expertise and continue delivering high-quality professional services. He has also contributed to the community as a volunteer with the City of Vancouver’s Community Tax Clinic, where he completed over 300 tax filings for low-income individuals and newcomers.

To complement his accounting background, Gangadhar has completed the IBM Data Analytics Essentials and IBM Data Visualization & Dashboard Essentials certificates, enhancing his ability to interpret financial data, create clear dashboards and communicate insights in a way that clients can easily understand.

Outside of his professional work, Gangadhar is passionate about financial education and empowering newcomers to Canada by helping them understand their tax obligations, available credits and financial rights.
