Meet Gangadhar Tandale

Gangadhar Tandale is the Accounting Manager at Prismledger Accounting Services, dedicated to supporting individuals, newcomers and small business owners with accurate and affordable accounting solutions.

His extensive experience includes roles at MNP LLP, Manning Elliott LLP, and Buckley Dodds CPA LLP, where he served diverse industries and handled tax, bookkeeping and audit matters.

Gangadhar also worked as a CRA Income Tax Collections Officer, giving him insider-level understanding of audits, garnishments, liens, collections and compliance.

He has completed IBM Data Analytics and IBM Data Visualization & Dashboard Essentials, improving his ability to interpret numbers and present clear insights to clients.

Gangadhar is currently pursuing the CPA designation to further enhance his professional knowledge.
